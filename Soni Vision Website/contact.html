Getting Here

Conveniently Located in
Cypress, Texas

Our clinic is located on the Northwest Freeway (US-290) in Cypress — easily accessible from Katy, Spring, The Woodlands, and throughout the Greater Houston area.

From Katy

Take I-10 East to US-290 East. Take the Mueschke Road exit and follow Northwest Freeway to our location. Approximately 20 minutes.

From The Woodlands / Spring

Take TX-249 South to US-290 West. We are located on the service road of the Northwest Freeway. Approximately 25 minutes.

Parking

Free parking is available in the building lot. No validation required.
