## SONI VISION — IMPROVEMENTS BY TAB

### Homepage
1. **Add a credibility stat bar to the hero** — Add a procedure volume stat ("Thousands of procedures performed" or specific count). Maloney uses "95,000+" which instantly conveys scale.
2. **Add a media/press logo bar** — Even 3–4 Houston press mentions (Houston Chronicle, local news, medical journals) in a strip below the hero. Maloney has 20 logos.
3. **Add award/credential badge logos** — Texas Monthly Top Doctors, ASCRS membership, AAO — displayed as visual badges. No visual trust signals currently exist on the homepage.
4. **Expand patient reviews section** — Currently shows 3 static text reviews. Add 6–8 minimum, and add "LEAVE A REVIEW" footer link on every page.
5. **Add blog post previews** — Both competitors display 3–4 recent posts on the homepage. Surface the blog here to drive traffic and signal activity.
6. **Add "Where do I begin?" patient journey cards** — 2–3 quick-answer cards addressing common pre-consultation hesitation ("Am I a candidate?", "What does it cost?", "What's recovery like?").
7. **Replace "Medical Ophthalmology" service card** — Weak, generic category. Replace with something more specific and conversion-focused (e.g., "Dry Eye Treatment") or remove to keep homepage focused on surgical strengths.
8. **Add a text/chat widget** — Maloney uses Podium for SMS. A "Text us" widget is a proven conversion tool for surgical practices.
9. **Add an embedded Instagram feed** — Both competitors embed their feed on the homepage. Signals an active, living practice.
10. **Add financing/affordability mention** — Neither "CareCredit" nor "FSA/HSA" appears on the homepage. One line removes the price-anxiety filter for prospective patients.
11. **Add language support mention** — Dr. Soni (Hindi/Gujarati), Dr. Reddy (Telugu). Houston has large South Asian communities. A small language line near the header meaningfully expands reach.
12. **Add a "Referring Providers" CTA** — AVC has dual patient/referring provider booking flows. This is important for OD referral network growth.
