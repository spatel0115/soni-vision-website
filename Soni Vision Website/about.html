Founder & Lead Surgeon

Dr. Ruhi Soni,
MD

Dr. Ruhi Soni is the founder of Soni Vision Institute and a board-certified ophthalmologist with more than 15 years of experience in cataract and refractive surgery. A Cypress, Texas native, she studied engineering at Texas A&M University before returning to Houston for medical school and residency at Baylor College of Medicine.

Recognized as a Rising Star by Texas Monthly and honored as a Women in Medicine recipient in 2022, Dr. Soni is known for her precision, compassionate care, and commitment to each patient's unique needs. She specializes in refractive cataract surgery, advanced technology lens implantation, and eyelid restoration.

Dr. Soni speaks Punjabi, Urdu, Hindi, and English, and serves a diverse patient community across Cypress, Katy, and Greater Houston.

Surgeon & Partner

Dr. Nikitha Reddy,
MD

Dr. Nikitha Reddy is a fellowship-trained ophthalmologist who brings advanced surgical expertise and a patient-first philosophy to Soni Vision Institute. She graduated summa cum laude from The University of Texas at Austin before earning her medical degree at UT Southwestern Medical School, where she also completed her ophthalmology residency — the nation's largest surgical training program.

Dr. Reddy went on to complete a prestigious fellowship in Glaucoma and Advanced Anterior Segment Surgery (GAASS) under world-renowned surgeon Dr. Ike Ahmed. She specializes in refractive cataract surgery, complex cataract cases, advanced technology IOLs, and minimally invasive glaucoma surgery (MIGS).

Dr. Reddy speaks Hindi, Telugu, and English, and is deeply trusted by her patients for her ability to combine clinical excellence with genuine compassion.
